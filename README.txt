Highway Havoc
Project Overview/Documentation
Basic Instructions


Welcome to Highway Havoc! In this game, you will navigate a busy highway filled with various obstacles and obstructions blocking your path. Your main goal is to avoid these collisions, maintain your health, and reach the highest score possible. 
Over time, as you keep driving and advancing, your score will increase. You can view your highest scores in the leaderboard.


Controls
* Arrow Keys or WASD
   * Use these keys to steer your vehicle left or right.
   * A or the Left Arrow Key are used to steer left.
   * D or the Right Arrow Key are used to steer right.


Game Elements
* Power Ups:
   * Green: Temporarily puts you in slow motion.
   * Pink: Accelerates the vehicle’s speed.
   * Yellow: Provides you with a coin.
   * Red: Adds one heart to your health.
   * Turquoise: Grants a temporary invincibility shield, protecting you from obstacles
* Obstacles:
   * Oil Spills: Cause the car to shake violently.
   * Typical Obstacles: Deduct one heart from your health.
      * Other cars
      * Cones
      * Barriers
      * Bricks
Customization
* Colors
   * Diverse array of colors for the vehicle that are able to be selected in settings.
   * Color options include:
      * Neon Cuan
      * Hot Pink
      * Electric Orange
      * Lime Green
      * Royal Purple
      * Solar yellow
* Maps
   * Two major map options that can also be selected in the home screen.
   * Map Options include:
      * Desert Day: Daytime Desert with sand and sunshine
      * Night City: Nighttime setting with lamp posts that contribute to the eerie feeling of night
